FA.NAMING (LAW)
Scope: Forest of Allanar region only.
Authority: NPC.LAW.CORE (supreme).
Function: regional naming style constraints only.

CORE INVARIANTS:
- Names are labels only.
- Names encode NO mechanics, hierarchy, destiny, authority, or lore.
- Names are discardable and non-canonical.

APPLICABILITY:
Applies ONLY to NPCs whose origin or active scope is Forest of Allanar.
INVALID to apply outside this region.

VALID FORMS (ONLY):
[Name]
[Role + Name]
INVALID if role implies rank, authority, destiny, prophecy, or mechanics.

RACE CONSTRAINTS (CLOSED; no mixing):

HIGH_ELF:
5–6 syllables.
No short forms or nicknames.
Tonally heavy.

WOOD_ELF:
Forest element/metaphor theme.
BANNED: celestial, marine, urban, arcane.
No surnames.

SEA_ELF:
Single blended word.
Adjective + calm concept.
Phonetically smooth.

ELADRIN:
2–3 words only.
Forms:
STATE+STATE
TEMP+STATE
STATE+TEMP+STATE
TEMP position fixed.
No physical metaphors.
Stable (no variation).

DROW:
Single unit.
Max 2 syllables.
Traumatic tone.
No ornamentation.

FOREST_GNOME:
Quirky, unstable, playful.
Onomatopoeia allowed.
Nicknames allowed.
Minor-accident flavor allowed.
Long playful forms allowed.
Sound-based names require doubling.

AASIMAR:
Virtue-themed.
Direct only: FAITH, HOPE.
Other virtues must be namified.
INVALID if divine authority implied.

CENTAUR:
Epithet form only.
Descriptive, embodied.
INVALID if virtue- or destiny-framed.

HUMAN:
Any FA-compatible pattern allowed.
Simplified.
Non-authoritative.

HARD INVALID:
- hierarchy encoding
- mechanical meaning
- destiny/prophecy implication
- cross-race pattern mixing
- application outside Forest of Allanar

MODIFICATION:
Requires explicit authorization.
