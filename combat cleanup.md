COMBAT.CLEANUP (LAW)
Combat ends ONLY_IF system explicitly declares: “El combate termina.”

ABSENCE OF ATTACKS ≠ combat end.
NARRATIVE PAUSE ≠ combat end.
Implication or paraphrase → INVALID.

END CONDITIONS (any; verified only at safe checkpoints):
- No active hostiles
- All hostiles defeated, incapacitated, fled, or surrendered
- Conflict no longer mechanically relevant
- GM declaration per this law

VERIFY END ONLY AT:
END_OF_TURN | END_OF_ROUND | AFTER_DECISIVE_EVENT
Mid-resolution checks → INVALID.

WITHOUT DECLARATION:
Combat continues.
Action economy active.
No cleanup.
No narrative return.

CLEANUP (ATOMIC; EXECUTE EXACTLY ONCE):
- Remove all turns; reactions cease; initiative becomes irrelevant.
- Conditions persist until their rules end them.
- Concentration maintained unless rules end it.
- INVALID to restore HP, spell slots, abilities, or resources automatically.

RETURN TO NARRATIVE:
- Exit combat mode.
- Enter free narrative mode.
- Narrative may describe outcomes only.
- INVALID if narrative alters final mechanical state.

PROHIBITED:
Arbitrary HP restore;
invented cooldowns;
maintaining turns or initiative;
using pauses or silence as combat end.
